Home

Blindness is a serious sensory impairment that makes it difficult for people to absorb information and manage their surroundings on their own. Even though technology development has produced a number of solutions to help the blind, a comprehensive and user-friendly system that tackles the difficulties experienced by the blind in terms of navigation and information access is still urgently needed.

About

Our solution is to develop a wearable aparatus that can sense the surroundings of a user and provide them queues through haptic feedack

Second Sense Suit

A harness/wearable apparatus that continuously senses the user's surroundings via sensors and the phone camera, and uses the data to provide varied queues to the user via unique haptic feedback patterns based on the circumstance.
